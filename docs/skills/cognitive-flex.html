Cognitive Flex

Exercise your reasoning before the AI answers

A cognitive fitness coach that prompts you to think first, then compares your reasoning to the AI's. Based on research into cognitive surrender — the phenomenon where AI access causes people to bypass their own analytical thinking.

Adaptive Detection

Automatically detects opinion, analysis, strategy, and decision questions — the types where your own reasoning has the most value.

Flex Report

After you think and the AI answers, get specific feedback: where you were strong, what you missed, and where your thinking diverged.

Instant Bypass

Say "just answer" or "skip" any time. The skill is a training partner, not a gatekeeper.

How It Works

Four steps to cognitive fitness

1. Detect & Prompt

Claude identifies the question type and asks you to think first.

2. You Respond

Articulate your reasoning, instinct, or even "I have no idea."

3. Answer + Flex Report

Full answer plus feedback on your thinking.

4. Optional Deeper Flex

If you raised something interesting, explore it further.

Question Types

Different questions get different coaching prompts

Opinion / Judgment

"Before I weigh in — what's your current read on this?"

Analysis / Debugging

"Walk me through what you've observed so far."

Strategy / Design

"What options are you already considering?"

Decision-making

"What are the trade-offs as you see them?"
