Dinner Party

Stage a debate between 40 thinkers to pressure-test your idea

Bring an idea to a table of 40 thinkers from across history. They debate from their own worldviews, blind spots, and intellectual style. The friction between them surfaces angles you wouldn't get from a single perspective.

Tagged Guest Roster

40 thinkers tagged by locale, era, domain, thinking style, and energy. Filter and combine to build the table you need.

Three Conversation Modes

Quick round for breadth, debate for friction, full dinner for the complete arc from positions to synthesis.

Smart Assembly

Auto-selects guests with productive friction. At least two domains, one skeptic, one builder, and direct relevance to your topic.
